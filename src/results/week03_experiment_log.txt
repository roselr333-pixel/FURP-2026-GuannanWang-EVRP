=== FAILURE CASES (constraint-level diagnosis) ===

FAILURE 1 — CVRP, 20 customers, 1 vehicle (capacity=100):
  feasible=False, violated constraint=CAPACITY (single truck cannot serve total demand 187 with cap 100)
  fix: increase fleet size or vehicle capacity; this is a modelling limit, not a code bug.

FAILURE 2 — VRPTW, 20 customers, time windows latest=30 (very tight):
  feasible=False, violated constraint=TIME WINDOWS (customers too far from depot to be served within 30 min)
  fix: widen time windows or add more vehicles / reduce service time.

FAILURE 3 — EVRP-TW, corridor instance, battery capacity = 40 (too small):
  feasible=False, violated constraint=ENERGY (vehicle cannot reach the farthest
  customer and return even with recharge, because battery < one-way leg energy).
  fix: increase battery capacity or add an intermediate charging station.
